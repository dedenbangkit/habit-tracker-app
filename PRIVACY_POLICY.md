# Privacy Policy

**Habit Counter**
**Last Updated: April 14, 2026**

## Overview

Habit Counter ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our mobile application.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.**

The App operates entirely offline and does not:
- Collect personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- Track your location
- Access your contacts, photos, or other device data
- Use analytics or tracking services
- Display advertisements
- Require account creation or login

## Data Storage

All data you create in the App (habits, progress, streaks, and settings) is stored **locally on your device only**. This includes:
- Habit names and configurations
- Daily progress counts
- Streak history
- App preferences (theme settings)

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any servers.

## Data Sharing

**We do not share any data with third parties** because we do not collect any data.

## Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you:
- Delete the App
- Use the "Reset All Data" option in Settings
- Clear the App's data through your device settings

## Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. The App is safe for users of all ages.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services that collect data.

## Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security measures. We recommend:
- Using a device passcode or biometric lock
- Keeping your device's operating system updated

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
